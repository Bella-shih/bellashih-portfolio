## Process

We started with hand-drawn lettering and storyboards in pencil, then translated them into After Effects. The palette was inspired by stage lighting — warm ambers, cool stage blues, and the unmistakable cream of the Tiny Desk itself.

### What we learned

A small character — even one made of three shapes — can carry an entire piece if the timing has confidence and the spacing breathes. We trimmed twenty seconds off our first cut and the package only got stronger.
