## The practice

Every weekday morning, I spend 15-20 minutes making one small drawing. Usually in Procreate, sometimes on paper. The only rules: it has to be finished, and it has to ship.

After a year of this, the drawings have gotten looser, faster, and (I think) more honest.

### What I've learned

The hardest part isn't drawing well — it's drawing every day. Showing up matters more than peaking.
