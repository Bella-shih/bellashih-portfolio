## Behind it

This started as a sketch in a coffee shop and slowly grew into a film. I wanted to see if I could tell a complete story using only the things on my own kitchen counter — a kettle, a cup, a lemon, a knife. No dialogue, no narration.

It taught me that restriction is generative. When you can't lean on words, you have to commit to motion.
