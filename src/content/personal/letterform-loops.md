## The why

I've always loved how typography can feel like a cast of characters. Each letter has its own posture, its own temperament. This series was an excuse to animate that idea — to let an 'a' be playful and a 'B' be stoic.

### Process

Worked entirely in After Effects with hand-drawn intermediate frames. The looping was the hardest part — getting motion that felt continuous without being mechanical.
