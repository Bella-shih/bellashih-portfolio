## What I'm learning

Rive's state machine model forces you to think about animation as a graph of states rather than a timeline — closer to game design than traditional motion. It's slower at first, but the payoff is animations that genuinely respond to people instead of just playing back.

### Next steps

- Build a full character rig with secondary animation
- Try wiring up sound effects to states
- Get good enough to deploy a Rive piece somewhere real
